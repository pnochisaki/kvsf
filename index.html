WHAT WE ARE

Knight Vision Productions is a custom event production service, woman-owned, based in San Francisco, California, specializing in worldwide marketing and promotional launches and activations, and both small and large scale events. We work in the industries of entertainment, technology, social activism and the fine arts.

MEGAN KNIGHT

FOUNDER

 

As an East Bay Area native, Megan grew up on a ranch run by four generations of women in Northern California. The work ethic stuck (rural life not so much) and she landed in New York City at 18, beginning her career in events. Megan now has well over a decade of large scale event planning and production leadership experience, having partnered with a venerable client list with unique project environments and design. Now commissioned worldwide and based in San Francisco, her free time is spent back at the ranch.
